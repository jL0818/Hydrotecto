HYDROTECTO is an IoT system that sends
early warning to its user when water reaches
the set particular unsafe level. With this
assistance, precautions and preparations
will be observed to keep the user warned
and safe.
It contains multiple features like safety
tips for when it's safe and unsafe, rainfall
detectiontips, access to Open Weather API, current weather can also be monitored when connected to internet.
