Steps:

Step 1:
The main screen shows the current status of the water level. If the water did not reach the sensor, the app will show a safe status on the screen. On the other hand, if the sensor detected water, it will prompt you an unsafe status and you have to take precautionary measures.

Step 2:
The Daily Weather tab is your access to current weather from Open Weather API which requires internet connection to get you updated.

Step 3:
At the upper left corner of the app is a striped icon as menu toggle where Home, Description, How To Use This Application, and Summary are listed.

Step 4:
Home menu shows the main screen of the app where detection happens.

Step 5:
Description is a quick overview of the of the whole system.

Step 6:
Logs is where all the recent detections are logged.
